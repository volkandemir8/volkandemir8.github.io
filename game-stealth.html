Skeleton Dungeon: Sneak & Survive

Descend into the darkest and most dangerous dungeons in this gripping tactical stealth adventure! Your mission is simple: grab the hidden treasures, outsmart the heavily armed guards, and reach the exit completely unseen. But surviving the shadows requires more than just staying out of sight.

Plan your every move carefully as you navigate through treacherous corridors. Use your arsenal of unique abilities to your advantage—turn invisible to slip past tight patrols, throw rocks into the dark to distract enemies, move with absolute silence, or unleash powerful stun bombs when you are cornered. Whether you prefer a slow, methodical approach or fast-paced evasion, your wits and reflexes will be tested to the absolute limit. Can you escape without leaving a trace?
